Structure Search

June 25th - July 25th, 2017

A considerably large project start the second I got out of school. I was always curious abot how companies worked, and wanted to know. Thus an app was born. By scraping the web, my algorithm finds the employees of a company, and then structures them into an estimate of said company's organizational structure. Currently there is a limit of 1000 employees for a company to work, but if I ever returned to the project, I might grow this value. *Please note this repository is private, however you may access the api and web app for free, at structuresearch.herokuapp.com
